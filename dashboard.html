Privacy Policy

BOQ Connect collects your Google account email and project data solely to generate and update your BOQ Google Sheet. No data is stored on SUN_SLDev servers. All project data lives in your own Google Drive.

Google authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser session only. You can sign out at any time to revoke access.
